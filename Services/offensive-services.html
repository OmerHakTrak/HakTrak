Mimic Real Cyberattacks

Our approach involves simulating real-world cyberattacks to identify your organization’s vulnerabilities and weaknesses, ensuring that you are prepared to face even the most sophisticated threats.

Uncover Hidden Gaps

Service Overview

Cybersecurity Compliance Services

At HakTrak, we offer comprehensive compliance services designed to safeguard your sensitive data and systems while minimizing the risk of cyberattacks and data breaches

Vulnerability Assessment

Our experts use automated tools to scan your systems and network for known vulnerabilities. We then assess the severity of these vulnerabilities to determine the level of risk they pose.

Penetration Testing

Our penetration testing involves simulated real-world attacks on your systems and networks. This thorough testing helps us identify vulnerabilities and weaknesses that could be exploited by malicious attackers.

Red Teaming

Through red teaming, we conduct realistic attacks on your defenses to uncover vulnerabilities that other testing methods might not detect. This provides a holistic view of your organization’s security posture.

Social Engineering Testing

We evaluate your defenses against social engineering attacks such as phishing. This testing helps identify areas where employees might inadvertently compromise security.
