In the early of 2019 a team of cybersecurity professionals sponsored up by visionary businesses men were driven by the rapid growth of e-services and digitalisation in the Saudi market, and the promising vision 2030, to establish the first Saudi cybersecurity R&D company to develop security products and solutions.

HakTrak was formed with client centric, cybersecurity focused, services oriented, and quality driven principles. We take pride in being the first Saudi company to develop its own threat hunting platform registered with Intellectual Property (IP), and became the first choice for businesses seeking top-notch cybersecurity solutions
